A Poem For Anagha ❤️

Anagha isn’t the type who tries to stand out she just naturally does… She’s different from everyone else… in the best way possible. I don’t think I’ve ever seen her be wrong, and honestly, I don’t even know how… Sometimes it feels like she isn’t even real 😭 but at the same time, she’s the most real person I’ve ever met!! She can’t fake anything… Her smile isn’t forced, and her vibes are never fake. There’s something so pure about her the way she talks, the way she cares, the way she simply exists…💖 Still, she tries her best to hide… to hide her pain and stay strong all the time, like she doesn’t want anyone to worry. She’s the kind of person you don’t fully understand at first, but the more you get to know her, the more you realize she’s something rare… something truly special. She’s like an angel, not because she’s perfect… though sometimes it feels like she is… 😅 but because her heart is. She’s the most beautiful woman in my life, not just for how she looks, but for who she is inside. And I’m really grateful to have her… someone who cares so deeply, loves so quietly, and carries such a pure soul. She feels unreal sometimes… but one thing I know for sure, she could never be fake. I love you Anagha…❤️
