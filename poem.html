A Poem For Anagha ❤️

In a world full of noise and rush,
You are my calm, my quiet hush.

Your smile lights my darkest days,
In a thousand beautiful ways.

Not just beauty that meets the eye,
But kindness that reaches the sky.

If hearts had homes, mine would stay,
Right beside yours every day.

— Shreyas ❤️
