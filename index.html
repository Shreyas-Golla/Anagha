Crazy how it all started
with a DT project.

Just another college group…

or at least
that's what I thought.

That day everyone
had already said good night.

But somehow…

our conversation
had just begun.

And somehow

one conversation
turned into hours.

I still don't know
how you managed to do that to me 😁

But suddenly,
sleep felt less important
than talking to you.

And somehow,
you casually became
the best part of my day.

And the more we talked,

the more I realized
how special you are.

Some stories start loudly.

Ours started quietly
with one late-night conversation.

And somehow,
it became
my favorite story.

I love you Anagha ❤️
